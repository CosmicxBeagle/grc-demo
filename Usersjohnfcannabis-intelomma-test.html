Administrative Actions

Overview

The Oklahoma Medical Marijuana Authority (OMMA) is committed to prioritizing public health and patient safety through routine inspections and thorough regulation of the state’s medical marijuana program.

This page serves as a public record of administrative actions filed against commercial licensees. Transparency in these proceedings promotes operational compliance with state law while keeping patients and the public informed of case developments.
